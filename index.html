Ezra Kenigsberg: The Man, the Myth, the Legend

An economics undergraduate, Ezra discovered he was way more intterested in messing with computers while working in Economic Consulting and has been steadily moving toward pure computer work ever since.

Ezra worked for salesforce.com starting in 2005, and pioneered the Data Architect role within their “Customers for Life” (CFL) consulting practice. He has since 2011 worked as a Salesforce Architect for employers in the Austin area, including Dell, Bazaarvoice, and BigCommerce.
